FAQ
When To Use The Different Types:
  • Administrative - Mainly used for internal meetings or internal emails
  • Account Mgmt - Non-technical work or meetings
  • Technical - All case work or technical meetings
  • Training - When attending a training session virtual or in-person
  • Side Projects - Anything non-customer related
  • PTO - PTO or Sick Time
When Do I Use Weekly Total to CSV?
  • Use this at the end of your last day of the work week.
When Do I Use Backup to CSV?
  • Use this nightly if you wish to backup your current days data in case of data loss overnight.
When Do I Upload To Box?
  • At the end of your work week, take the Weekly Total file and upload to your Box folder.
When Do I Clear Current?
  • You should clear current items at the start of a new business day. This removes all items that are displayed but not the weekly totals.
When Do I Clear All?
  • You should clear all items at the beginning of a new work week. This will clear out all saved data!
